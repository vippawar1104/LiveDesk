How It Works

Three steps to a live session.

01

Connect

Click Connect and grant microphone access. Your session starts immediately with bi-directional audio.

02

Share

Optionally turn on your camera or share your screen so the assistant can see your context.

03

Converse

Talk, type, or both. The assistant keeps answers short, asks clarifying questions, and stays grounded.
